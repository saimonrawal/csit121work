I am Saimon Rawal and I am a student at CCBC Essex studying information technology. Before taking CSIT 121, I did not have much experience building websites, and most of what I knew came from using websites rather than creating them.

In this course, I learned how web pages are built using HTML for structure and CSS for style and layout. I learned how to use headers, navigation menus, sections, and images to organize content correctly. This class also helped me understand why accessibility and responsive design matter. It changed the way I look at websites and helped me understand how they work behind the scenes.
